Ghost Bubbles

A Program that creates little pink translucent bubbles and sends them off in random direction towards the lower right hand corner, and they will travel at random speeds. It's very relaxing to look at and fun to mess with. Occasionally when you change to bounce some of the balls that were midtranistion will get caught, try to see how mny of them you can get at a time.

Contols

Click The mouse to create a new ghostly bubble
Press the Left and Right keys to swap between wrapping around the edge and bouncing off of it.
To play Simply Click on the Image.
