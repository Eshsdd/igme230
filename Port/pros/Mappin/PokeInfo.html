Pokemon Grey

A Pokemon Safari like Game I made in java Script, and was my final project my freshman year. I was really ambitious and wanted a variety of pokemon, animations, and levels all the trimmings you could say. However, much like this project I was running out of time, regardless I had the all the requirements down pat. I wasn't worried, after all I'm someone who can (barring other obligations) can get things that need to be done, done.

Controls

Use the Arrow keys to Move
Click on the options with your mouse once you enter a battle screen.
To play Simply Click on the Image.
