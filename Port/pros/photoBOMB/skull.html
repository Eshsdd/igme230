Photo Skull

It's a color inverted image of my skull Icon, you can change the color back but it's sporadic and looks glitchy or possibly pixelated? Depends on who you ask, and it's not an easy task to make a good looking skull in this program. However, it sure is fun! So try your best, I'm rooting for you!

Instructions

As your mouse hovers over the image it creates small blocks made of the inversed color of the current color on the screen.
The Top left corner has an example of what the final product could look like.
To play Simply Click on the Image.
