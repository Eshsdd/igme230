Ball Gobbler

A program I made in JavaScript, the object of the game is to eat all the balls. It's not that difficult as there are no real ways to lose. There's a small counter in the bottom right hand corner, and when you start getting to real high numbers the balls will double up making you go across twice.

Contols

To move tap the Left, Right, Up, and Down keys.
To restart Press Spacebar.
To play Simply Click on the Image.
